About Summit

About The Bharat Trilogy Summit

The Bharat Trilogy Summit, an esteemed event dedicated to the Viksit Bharat 2047 mission. This summit focuses on empowering small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and fostering meaningful connections with investors to drive India's economic future. Powered by us, the summit will take place on November 29, 2025 (Saturday) in Mumbai

Goal: A high-impact gathering of investors & to create a roadmap for financial excellence, anchored in Indorient's commitment to values driven services.

A Unique Networking & Fund Raise Event Framework Drawing Inspiration & Blending With Our Ancient Wisdom.

With stretch of imagination, this in-person summit in Mumbai, connecting Bharat's Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) with institutional investors through a first-of-its-kind elevator pitch model supporting our SMEs.

Select SMEs will deliver 5-minute pitches to secure funding, showcasing their innovation and growth potential.

Featuring an engaging chat show with a special guest on Making Wealth in 2025 and beyond, the event blends ancient Indian wisdom with modern investment strategies in a trust-building environment.

Tailored for 100 high-profile investors, followed by Sundown Snacks & Drinks, this summit unveils a new partnership and fosters transformative investment opportunities, aligning with India's Atma Nirbhar Bharat vision.

About
The Bharat Trilogy Summit

As India marches toward its visionary goal of Viksit Bharat 2047—emphasizing inclusive and sustainable development—SMEs stand as pivotal engines of economic transformation. Recognizing this, we have taken proactive steps to empower Bharat ke SMEs, culminating in this unique investment summit.

Key Theme:

  • Ethical Leadership for Viksit Bharat:Emphasizing fair trade and stakeholder welfare to build enduring trust with clients and investors, aligning with India’s 2047 goal of equitable prosperity.
  • Community-Driven Ecosystems:Reimagining the ancient Shreni model to foster SME networks that prioritize local empowerment, quality, and sustainability, contributing to Viksit Bharat’s inclusive growth agenda.
  • Profit with Purpose:Embedding Dharma to harmonize financial success with social and environmental impact, ensuring SMEs play a vital role in shaping a developed, harmonious nation by 2047.
  • Resilient Innovation:Inspired by India’s storied trade networks, this theme encourages SMEs to innovate boldly while remaining rooted in cultural values, enhancing global competitiveness.
